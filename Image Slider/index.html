UCHIHA MADARA

Madara Uchiha was the legendary leader of the Uchiha clan. He is also known as Ghost of uchiha.

KATSUKI BAKUGO

Katsuki Bakugo, also known as Kacchan by his childhood friends and his hero name Great Explosion Murder God Dynamight, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School, training to become a Pro Hero.

UCHIHA ITACHI

Itachi Uchiha was a shinobi of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan who served as an Anbu Captain.

LEVI ACKERMAN

Levi Ackerman, often formally referred to as Captain Levi , is the squad captain of the Special Operations Squad within the Survey Corps and is widely known as humanity's strongest soldier.

RYOUMEN SUKUNA

Ryomen Sukuna, more often called just Sukuna, is a mighty cursed spirit known as the undisputed King of Curses. He serves as one of the primary antagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series.

GOJO SATORU

Satoru Gojo is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest in the world. Satoru is the pride of the Gojo Family, the first person to inherit both the Limitless and the Six Eyes in four hundred years.
